Chicken Curry

Description

A rich, creamy, and comforting curry with tender chicken simmered in coconut milk + warm spices.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces. Thighs/drumsticks stay juicier
  • 1 large onion diced
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger grated
  • 1 potato cubed
  • 1 carrot sliced
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmic
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt + Pepper to paste
  • 1 can 400ml coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water or chicken broth
  • 2 tomatoes chopped
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
Instructions
  1. Pat chicken dry, season with salt + pepper. Cut all veggies so cooking is smooth
  2. Heat oil on medium-high. Brown chicken 3-4 mins per side. Don't cook through. Set aside.
  3. Same pan, add onion. Cook 4mins till translucent. Add garlic + ginger, cook 1min till fragrant
  4. Add curry powder, turmeric, cumin, chill powder. Stir 30sec. This wakes up the flavor.
  5. Add tomatoes + potatoes + carrots. Pour coconut milk + water. Return chicken + juices to pan.
  6. Bring to boil, then reduce to low summer. Cover and cook 20-25 mins till chicken is tender and sauce thickens. Stir occasionally so it doesn't stick.
  7. Taste + adjust salt. Squeeze lemon/lime before serving.

Tips

  • Use thighs over breast. Breast dries out faster.
  • Mash 2-3 potato cubes against the pot side, or simmer uncoveres last 5 mins.
  • Marinate chicken 30 mins with 1 tsp curry powder + yogurt if you have time.
  • Start with 1/4 tsp chili powder, add more at the end. Easy to add, hard to remove.
